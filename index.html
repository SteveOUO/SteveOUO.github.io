Yixin Zheng | 郑一新

Ph.D. student working on Embodied AI and Robot Learning.

I am a Ph.D. student in Control Theory and Control Science, jointly trained by the Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences and Beijing Academy of Artificial Intelligence. I am affiliated with the EPIC Lab at Peking University.

My research focuses on world action models, vision-language-action models, robot foundation models, representation learning, 3D vision, and dexterous manipulation.

Advisors and mentors: Dr. Zhizheng Zhang, Prof. He Wang, and Prof. Xiaoguang Zhao.
